What is Movim?

A powerful social platforms for XMPP

When you setup Movim, it gives you a complete social and chat experience for the decentralized XMPP network.

You can easily connect to several XMPP servers at the same time.

You can also set it up to work with just one XMPP server to create a fully private social platform for you and your friends. Movim is fully compatible with the most used XMPP servers such as ejabberd or Prosody.
